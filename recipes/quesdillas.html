Quesadillas

Description

These cheesesteak quesadillas are a family-favorite meal in my household.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 small onions, sliced
  • 2 medium green bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce (such as Bull's-EyeÂ® Texas-Style Bold Barbeque Sauce)
  • 8 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Cook and stir beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Add onions and bell peppers; cook and stir until softened, 5 to 10 minutes. Pour barbeque sauce over beef mixture and simmer until sauce is slightly reduced, about 10 minutes.
  3. Lay 4 tortillas on a baking sheet; top each with beef mixture, Cheddar cheese, and remaining tortillas.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Flip quesadillas and cook until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes more.
